in pursuit of happiness


I confess that I'm guilty of wanting to be happy all the time and with that intent I've enthusiastically tried my hand in whatever that came my way, with a hope that it may make me happy [for whatever length of time]. In this process I've acquainted myself with a good skill set [not to say that I haven't terribly flopped at some things- I really suck at chess] and a large number of interests. I must say, at this point, I almost know nothing in most of it, but I do know something and that makes me happy.
Public Speaking

My love for public speaking was born from my interest to present ideas. I fought really hard to be able to discuss as freely as I can today and a lot of my ease is because of my friends back home.

Writing

Writing is perhaps my single skill I'm neither boastful off nor disappointed in. I only hope to get better. I love writing stories, rants, scripts and poems. The satisfaction I get after finishing a piece is unmatched.

Web Development

My interests lie more in web design than in web development. I have a good working knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript essential for front-end development. For design, I shamefully use a few libraries-materialize and bootstrap and customise within. It has taken me quite sometime to come to a stage where I can be confident of assimilating these skills to build a website.

Java

I was introduced to computer programming in High School through JAVA. JAVA was my focus for almost 3 years then, which resulted in a final project- where in I built a customised solitaire game. I am fairly familiar with Object Oriented Programming.

Arduino

My interest in Hardware Modelling started in my Freshman Year of Engineering when I attended a workshop in the winters of 2016. It grew further when I played around with a bunch of sensors as a part of General Championships. I also participated in B.R.I.C.K.S a semi autonomous bot competition organised by Robotix Society during Kshitij.

Python

I have a basic knowledge of python acquired during Summer of 2017 as a part of an online course. Used python mainly to implement flask for backend of a project. The ease offered by python, will probably make me switch over to python for further interests.

Aerospace Engineering

I have a love-hate relationship with Aerospace Engineering. I have found my love in Aerodynamics and am actively pursuing aerodynamic design-related projects.

Dramatics

As a part of Technology Dramatics Society-Encore I've entered into the world of stage-acting. I've been a part of five stage plays, a mime and recently even ventured into directorial roles. If not a better actor, I've certainly become a more empathetic person.

Film-making

Film is the most powerful form of expression in this age and its comical how obliviously we walk around with cameras in our pockets. Technology Filmmaking and Photography Society[TFPS] has shook me out of that oblivion and introduced me to the bliss that filmmaking is.
